Animated GIF-like feature

The user can define a series of facelet definitions and a step rate. Then the simulator will automatically cycle through each facelet definition based on the user's determined step rate. Upon reaching the end step, the simulator cycles back to step 1 and continues to repeat. This could be useful for displaying the steps of a cube solving method in a single simulator, cycling through interesting cube patterns, etc. Modification of the AnimCubeJS program is not needed in this case.
